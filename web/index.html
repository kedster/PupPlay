ℹ️ About PupPlay

🎮 Features

  • Multiple Pet Species: Dogs, Cats, Birds, Fish, Hamsters, Rabbits
  • AI Behaviors: Pets interact autonomously with unique personalities
  • Story Mode: 4 chapters with progressive quests and AI-driven events
  • Pet Shop: Purchase pets, food, toys, and supplies
  • House Management: Upgrade your home and increase pet capacity
  • Progression System: Level up, earn money, unlock content
